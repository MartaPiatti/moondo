OUR JOURNEY

Founded in 2030, Moondo travel agency aims to create new experiences and to show its costumers the same old cities under a completely new light. Starting from the big impact that the global warming has had on our planet, Moondo decided to seize the new possibilities that the new reality we live in has to offer us. By offering different activities, related to the sea level, we want to create extraordinary experiences giving you a once in a lifetime adventure.

We guarantee a wide range of activities and different destinations where you can experience a extraordinary vacation, as well as support to organize it.

OUR BIGGEST PARTNER

Our scientists have been tracking the Moon cicle to allow us to spot the best destinations for your next vacations. The lunar orbit makes an oscillation which takes 18.6 years to complete. In the first half of this cycle, the difference between the maximum and minimum tidal peaks is reduced. The coincidence between the effects of the global warming and the Moon entering her second half of the circle is what generated the floods that allow the existence of Moondo.
